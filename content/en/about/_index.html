plusserver Kubernetes Engine (PSKE)

{{< /blocks/section >}} {{< blocks/section >}}
The plusserver Kubernetes Engine (PSKE) is a Kubernetes orchestration solution offered by plusserver that enables users to quickly and easily orchestrate their Kubernetes clusters in self-service. With PSKE, users can run their workloads in DSGVO-compliant pluscloud open or on AWS, depending on their requirements, and manage them via a central interface. Plusserver handles the management of Garden Cluster, Seed Cluster and Etcd backups for its customers. The PSKE also provides a variety of features that relieve Day 2 operations, such as automated patching of worker nodes, Kubernetes patch version update and rolling upgrade of Kubernetes minor version at the push of a button. PSKE provides users with high flexibility and independence from individual cloud providers and multi-cloud capability.
- PSKE provides a fast and easy way to orchestrate self-service Kubernetes clusters.
- The PSKE is GDPR compliant and offers flexibility in choosing where to operate workloads.
- The PSKE supports multi-cloud deployment and allows the choice between different cloud providers.
- The PSKE allows users to manage their Kubernetes clusters from a central interface, either via a graphical interface or via API.
- Plusserver takes over the management of the Garden Cluster, Seed Cluster and etcd backups for its customers.
- The PSKE offers integrated Day 2 operations such as automated patching of the worker nodes and updating of the Kubernetes patch version.
- The PSKE offers an integrated lifecycle management for Kubernetes versions and worker operating systems.
- The PSKE allows users to roll out manifests to Kubernetes clusters running on other OpenStack platforms, providing independence from individual cloud providers.
- The PSKE is part of the pluscloud open ecosystem, which ensures data protection compliance and independence from individual cloud providers.
- With the PSKE, users can relieve their DevOps teams and free up space for their digital innovations.
