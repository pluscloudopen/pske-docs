plusserver Kubernetes Engine (PSKE) is a managed Kubernetes solution that allows you to quickly and easily orchestrate Kubernetes clusters in a self-service manner. With PSKE, you can operate and centrally manage your workloads in a multi-cloud environment, including hyperscalers and the GDPR-compliant pluscloud open in Germany. PSKE offers integrated day 2 operations and optional professional services, enabling your DevOps teams to focus on application development. You benefit from high resilience and optimized costs through autoscaling and hibernation, and the platform is designed with security and data protection in mind, following GDPR principles.
